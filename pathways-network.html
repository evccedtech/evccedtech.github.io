Pathways at EvCC

The following network graph shows proposed pathways at Everett Community College.

Top-level nodes are the pathways themselves. Second-level nodes are groups of programs within a given pathway. And program nodes indicate the degree or certificate program a student might choose. Some programs are placed in multiple pathways.

Click and drag on any node to reposition it within the network. This can make it easier to see where pathways are connected based on shared programs.
