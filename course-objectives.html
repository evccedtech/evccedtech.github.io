Top Verbs in Course Learning Objectives at EvCC

This diagram shows the co-occurrence of the top ten verbs used in course learning objectives at Everett Community College. It displays the frequency with which each of these verbs appears with the other verbs across all courses. This visualization can be used to see whether certain verbs tend to be clustered together in courses, indicating a possible connection between the types of learning they measure.

To focus on a specific verb, hover over its label. This will adjust the visualization to show only the number of times that verb appears in course learning objectives with each of the other top verbs.
